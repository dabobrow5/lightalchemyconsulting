Composite TMI Score — Baseline

Measurable culture intelligence — mapped to the hierarchy of human need.

The Team Maslow Index maps your organization's engagement, culture, and fulfillment data onto Maslow's five-rung hierarchy — producing a 0–100 composite score that tells you not just where you are, but exactly where to focus.

TMI eliminates the "education sell" with leaders. It makes abstract culture work feel measurable, ROI-tied, and boardroom-ready. The core framing: org growth and individual growth are aligned. Both advance together.

TMI™ — Team Maslow Index

Org-wide and team-level culture baseline, composite scoring across all five rungs, and improvement pathway design for every level and role.

IMI™ — Individual Maslow Index

Individual-level companion to TMI. Maps personal fulfillment, purpose, and self-actualization — comparable to StrengthsFinder but oriented toward human flourishing. Deployed B2B within enterprise programs and available direct to individuals.
